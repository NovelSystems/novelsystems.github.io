The Company

Novel Systems Engineering, LLC

Novel Systems Engineering is a Portland, Oregon-based research and intellectual property development firm. The company's primary focus is the development, validation, and licensing of DDR-based polarimetric LiDAR sensor technology for maritime search and rescue, drone detection, and radar augmentation applications.

The firm operates on a pure IP licensing model: technology is developed and patented in-house, then licensed to defense and commercial OEMs for integration into production systems. Novel Systems Engineering does not manufacture sensor hardware.

Active pursuits include SBIR Phase I funding for DDR validation, provisional patent applications covering the core measurement method and hardware architecture, and initial licensing conversations with maritime OEMs.
