The Company

Novel Systems Engineering, LLC

Novel Systems Engineering, LLC is a Portland, Oregon IP development and licensing firm. The company develops, validates, and licenses DDR-based polarimetric LiDAR sensor technology for maritime search and rescue, drone detection, and radar augmentation applications. Technology is developed and patented in-house, then licensed to defense and commercial OEMs. The company does not manufacture sensor hardware.

Current activity: provisional patent prosecution covering the core DDR measurement method and hardware architecture, and licensing outreach to maritime OEMs.
